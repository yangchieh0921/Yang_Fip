Air Jordan is a brand of basketball shoes, athletic, casual, and style clothing produced by Nike.

It was created for former NBA player and 6 time NBA Finals MVP Michael Jordan. The original Air Jordan sneakers were produced exclusively for Michael Jordan in early 1984, and released to the public in late 1984
